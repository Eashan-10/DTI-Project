How to Play

1. Setup: Enter your name, then choose your preferred category and difficulty level.

2. Time Limit: Keep an eye on the clock! You have a limited amount of time to answer each question.

3. Skipping: If you get stuck, you can use the skip button to move forward, but you will not earn points for that question.

4. Winning: Score as high as you can to secure the top spot at the leaderboard!
